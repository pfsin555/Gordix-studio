## 2025-05-14 - [Navigation & Accessibility]
**Learning:** In a multi-tool static site, users can feel "trapped" in a sub-tool if there's no clear way to return to the dashboard. Additionally, icon-only buttons (like language toggles or visibility switches) are inaccessible to screen readers without ARIA labels.
**Action:** Always provide a clear "Home" or "Back" navigation element in headers and ensure all interactive icons have descriptive ARIA labels.
